Commited to Sustainability

We are committed to the wellbeing of society and preservation of the environment, while addressing the economic expectations of our shareholders. We are a leading manufacturer of plastic packaging, specializing in innovative solutions tailored for the industrial and food sectors, meeting their growing demands. We operate our business guided by our vision, mission, and values, ensuring compliance with relevant laws, regulations, and industry standards.

Circular Economy

We continue to work closely with our customers and value chain partners to support them in achieving their own circular economy commitments – to increase recycled content and design their products for recyclability.

Global Challenges

We are committed to addressing global challenges through proactive initiatives and sustainable practices. We prioritize environmental stewardship, integrating efficient resource management and reducing our carbon footprint. Our commitment extends to fostering social responsibility, promoting diversity, inclusion, and supporting community welfare initiatives. Economically, we strive for responsible growth, contributing to sustainable development and resilience. Through these efforts, we aim to make a positive impact on the world, driving meaningful change in the sectors we operate in and beyond.

Sustainability Policy

Our sustainability policy is built upon our commitment to operating responsibly and ethically. We strive to minimize our environmental impact, promote social equity, and ensure economic viability. By integrating sustainable practices into our daily operations, we aim to contribute positively to the planet and the communities we serve, while adhering to the highest standards of governance and accountability.

Responsible Care®

Responsible care encompasses our commitment to managing our operations in a manner that prioritizes safety, health, environmental stewardship, and community well-being. It reflects our dedication to transparent communication, compliance with regulations, and continuous improvement in all aspects of our business practices.

Social Resposibility

Social responsibility guides our efforts to contribute positively to society, beyond our business goals. It involves initiatives aimed at enhancing community well-being, promoting diversity and inclusion, supporting local economies, and addressing societal challenges. We prioritize ethical practices, transparency, and accountability in our interactions with stakeholders, aiming to make a meaningful impact and foster sustainable development
