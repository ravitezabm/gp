Commited to Sustainability

We are committed to the wellbeing of society and preservation of the environment, while addressing the economic expectations of our shareholders. We are a leading petrochemical company that provides innovative and differentiated polyolefin solutions and we conduct our business within the framework of our values, vision and mission, and in accordance with applicable laws, regulations and industry standards.

Circular Economy

We continue to work closely with our customers and value chain partners to support them in achieving their own circular economy commitments – to increase recycled content and design their products for recyclability.
