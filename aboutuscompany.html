About Us
Gulf Plastic Industries stands as the market leader in the production of flexible intermediate bulk containers (FIBC), polypropylene woven sacks, FFS Bags (FFS heavy-duty PE Film), and plastic disposable products. Our unwavering commitment to continuous product innovation, coupled with the utilization of advanced manufacturing technologies, ensures that we consistently deliver superior quality products to our valued customers. With a relentless pursuit of excellence, we strive to push the boundaries of innovation, setting new industry standards and exceeding customer expectations at every turn.

Strategically located within the Rusayl Industrial Estate in Muscat - a short distance from the major airport and seaport - Gulf Plastic Industries has capitalised upon its position as Oman's exclusive producer of flexible intermediate bulk containers (also known as jumbo or bulk bags) to develop into a prominent local and international supplier of world-class plastic products. The company, which commenced its opera- tions in 1998, boasts an impressive track record of innovation and with a facility equipped with machinery sourced from world-leading European suppliers, has succeeded in developing a diverse product portfolio which conforms to the most stringent international standards.
